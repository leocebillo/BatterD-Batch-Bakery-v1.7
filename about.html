Our Story

Bonding between two, their determination, and their hard-work.

Hardwork, dedication, and fun

Batter'd Batch Bakery started with a simple conversation between Cate and Leo. Cate spoke about previously baking cupcakes and cakes, and Leo suggested starting a business for it. From there, Batter'd Batch Bakery was born. with Cate baking delicious treats from her home kitchen, and Leo managing the administrative side of things. The two worked hard to ensure a smooth start to the business, focusing on quality, care, and friendly service.

Every order is made fresh in batches of 6 or 12, using quality ingredients and thoughtful detail. From classic flavors to custom cupcakes and cakes-- each treat is baked with love and care. Oppurtunities for cusstom orders will fall soon, so stay tuned!

As a home-based bakery, we focus on ensuring our creations are good quality, made with care, and delivered with a smile. From our home-made website, to our cupcakes and cakes, everything is made with that special touch of love.
